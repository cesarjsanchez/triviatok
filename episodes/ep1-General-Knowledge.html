Question 1: What is the longest river in Europe?

Answer: Volga River

Question 2: Which painter is known for his series of paintings featuring water lilies?

Answer: Claude Monet

Answer: 1890s (first modern Olympic Games held in 1896)

Answer: Mercury

Question 5: Which famous scientist developed the three laws of motion?

Answer: Sir Isaac Newton

Answer: Palindrome

Answer: Arizona

Question 8: Which philosopher wrote the book 'The Republic'?

Answer: Plato

Answer: Martin Luther King Jr.

Answer: Aldous Huxley

Question 19: Which element is represented by the chemical symbol 'Au'?

Answer: Gold

Answer: Ludwig van Beethoven
